"HORRID-HORDE"

Amidst a foreboding castle, players confront a diverse array of zombies, each with unique abilities and weaknesses. Navigating through dimly lit corridors, they scavenge for resources and uncover hidden secrets to outmaneuver the relentless undead horde. Survival demands strategic thinking and quick reflexes as players fight their way to freedom through the labyrinthine fortress of fear.

"Scene Creation/Shader Graph"

In a lush forest, a shimmering river flows with dynamic shader graph effects, its surface rippling gently in the sunlight. Post-processing magic enhances the scene with soft bloom, vibrant colors, and dynamic depth of field, drawing players into a tranquil oasis of natural beauty. As they stand on the riverbank, the sights and sounds of nature come alive, inviting them to pause and immerse themselves in this enchanting moment.

"Blitz Endless waves"

Dive into the depths of Aqua Rush, an adrenaline-fueled 3D endless runner game set in a mesmerizing underwater world. Players surf atop waves in an endless ocean teeming with life, navigating through dynamic obstacles such as coral reefs, underwater caverns, and treacherous whirlpools. However, the real challenge lies in facing off against a diverse array of water creatures acting as enemies.

"Zungle Town"

Welcome to "ZungleTown," My debut creation as a game developer! In this low-graphic adventure, players explore a jungle and a deserted town infested with zombies. With simple yet engaging graphics, immerse yourself in the eerie atmosphere of survival. Encounter basic zombies with tattered clothes and hungry eyes as you scavenge for resources and weapons to fend off the undead.
